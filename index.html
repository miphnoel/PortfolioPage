About Me

After graduating from George Washington University with a degree in Music and a minor in Economics, I spent several years performing professionally in and around NYC. Though my passion for music never waned, my desire to continue growing and moving forward precipitated a major change.

I dove into coding seeking a challenge that would allow me to be both analytical and creative, and to work as part of a team. Programming has become my greatest source of satisfaction and frustration, my intellectual sustenance and creative passion. I enjoy every step of the process, from embracing a new task and constructing a plan, to investigating stubborn bugs and optimizing efficiency. The gratification of seeing what I've built can only be topped by my excitement to keep making it better.
